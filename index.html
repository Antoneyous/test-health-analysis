Avg. Score ?Mean of Task N score % across all attempts that have a score for this task (including zeros). High (>85%) or very low (<15%) may indicate calibration issues.
Discrimination ?Pearson r between this task's score and the candidate's total score. Higher is better — it means the task reliably separates strong from weak candidates. Values below 0.2 may indicate a poor discriminator.
N ?Number of attempts that received this specific task variant.
Weight ?The percentage weight assigned to this task slot. All slot weights should sum to 100%.
